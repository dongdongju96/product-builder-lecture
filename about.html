About This Guide

This website was created as a practical guide and demonstration of Google's principles for creating a high-quality site. The goal is to provide a clear, actionable resource for creators who want to build a successful, user-focused website that meets AdSense program policies.

The content here is a synthesis of official advice from the Google AdSense blog, designed to be easy to understand and apply to your own projects. By following these principles, you can create a better experience for your users and a more sustainable online business.

Our philosophy is simple: Focus on the user, and all else will follow.
